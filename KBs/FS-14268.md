# Knowledge Base Entry: Wholesale Role-Based Prices Ignored in Cart Due to YayExtra Lite Plugin Conflict

## 🎯 Executive Summary
**The Issue:** Wholesale role-based prices are correctly configured on product settings pages but the cart always displays retail (regular) prices — caused by a conflict with the YayExtra Lite – WooCommerce Extra Product Options plugin, which overrides the price resolved by Wholesale Prices Premium.
**The Fix:** Disable the YayExtra Lite plugin, or contact YayExtra support to investigate why their plugin overrides role-based pricing from Wholesale Prices Premium.

---

## 🛠 Final Resolution (The "2nd Me" Guide)
> [!TIP]
> **Use this solution if a customer asks about:** wholesale price not applying in cart, correct price on product page wrong in cart, role-based pricing ignored at checkout, cart shows retail price for wholesale customer, extra product options conflict pricing
>
> **Steps to solve:**
> 1. Confirm the symptom: wholesale prices appear correctly on the product edit page but the cart/checkout shows retail price for wholesale-role customers.
> 2. Check whether YayExtra Lite (or YayExtra Pro) – WooCommerce Extra Product Options is installed and active.
> 3. To confirm the conflict, temporarily disable YayExtra Lite and add a product to the cart as a wholesale user — if the correct wholesale price appears, YayExtra is the cause.
> 4. **Resolution options:**
>    - **Option A:** Disable YayExtra Lite permanently if the extra product options functionality is not essential.
>    - **Option B:** Contact YayExtra support and report that their plugin overrides `woocommerce_product_get_price` or equivalent hooks, causing it to always return the retail price regardless of user role. Request a fix or compatibility setting from them.
> 5. There is currently no official integration between Wholesale Prices Premium and YayExtra Lite; wholesale support for this combination is limited.

## 📝 Sanitized Interaction History

**[CUSTOMER]:**
We are experiencing a critical issue with pricing management. Prices configured in the product settings (role-based pricing section) are correct, but in the cart and checkout completely incorrect prices are displayed. The behavior seems inconsistent — as if the plugin is ignoring role-based pricing or applying unexpected pricing logic. This affects all products in a specific category and impacts B2B customers on a live production site.

**[SUPPORT_AGENT]:**
After investigating the site, the issue is caused by the third-party plugin **YayExtra Lite – WooCommerce Extra Product Options**. There is currently no official integration with this plugin, so our support is limited. When the conflicting plugin is enabled, the cart always refers to the retail price.

I created a test product and confirmed: with YayExtra Lite active, the cart ignores the wholesale role price and uses retail. With it disabled, wholesale pricing works correctly.

The only workaround at this time is to disable the plugin or contact YayExtra's support team to investigate why it overrides Wholesale Prices role-based pricing.

## 💡 Internal Insights (If applicable)
- YayExtra Lite (WooCommerce Extra Product Options) likely hooks into price calculation filters at a higher priority than Wholesale Prices Premium, causing it to return a static/base price that bypasses role-based pricing logic.
- This is a confirmed conflict identified by the agent directly on the customer's site. No fix has been released on the Wholesale Suite side.
- If the customer needs both plugins, the path forward is YayExtra support — Wholesale Suite does not have an official compatibility layer for YayExtra.
- When diagnosing "correct price on product edit, wrong price in cart" symptoms, always check for extra product options or price modifier plugins as a first step.
