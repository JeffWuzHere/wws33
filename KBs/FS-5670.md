# Knowledge Base Entry: Applying Coupon Resets Wholesale Price to Retail in Cart

## 🎯 Executive Summary
**The Issue:** When a wholesale customer applies a coupon in the cart, the item price switches from the wholesale price to the retail price, and the coupon discount is then applied to that retail price instead.
**The Fix:** This was a confirmed bug introduced in Wholesale Prices v2.2.4. It was fixed in the subsequent release. Updating the Wholesale Prices plugin to the latest version resolves the issue.

---

## 🛠 Final Resolution (The "2nd Me" Guide)
> [!TIP]
> **Use this solution if a customer asks about:** coupon resets wholesale price, coupon shows retail price, coupon applied to wrong price, wholesale discount not applying with coupon, cart price wrong after coupon, advanced coupons wholesale conflict
>
> **Steps to solve:**
> 1. Confirm the behavior: wholesale price is correctly shown in cart before coupon is applied, but switches to retail price once a coupon code is entered.
> 2. Ask the customer what version of the Wholesale Prices (free) plugin they are running.
> 3. If they are on v2.2.4, instruct them to **update the Wholesale Prices plugin to the latest version** — the fix was released in the version immediately following v2.2.4.
> 4. After updating, ask the customer to test applying a coupon as a wholesale customer to confirm the wholesale price is preserved.
> 5. If the customer cannot update immediately, the temporary workaround is to roll back to v2.2.3 (the last unaffected version).
