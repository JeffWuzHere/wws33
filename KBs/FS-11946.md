# Knowledge Base Entry: Wholesale General Discount Mapping Shows Fixed Instead of Percentage on Product Editor

## 🎯 Executive Summary
**The Issue:** When a wholesale role has a percentage-based general discount configured via discount mapping, the product editor's individual product page always displays the discount type as "Fixed" rather than "Percentage," making it appear the mapping is not working.
**The Fix:** This is expected behavior — the product editor always defaults to showing "Fixed" for individual wholesale price fields, but the general percentage discount is still correctly applied on the frontend for logged-in wholesale customers.

---

## 🛠 Final Resolution (The "2nd Me" Guide)
> [!TIP]
> **Use this solution if a customer asks about:** wholesale discount showing fixed instead of percentage, general discount mapping not working, percentage discount appears as fixed on product page, wholesale role discount type wrong, discount mapping not reflected on product
>
> **Steps to solve:**
> 1. Confirm the customer has set up a general discount (percentage) under WooCommerce > Wholesale Prices > General Discount tab for their wholesale role.
> 2. Explain that the individual product editor always displays "Fixed" as the default state for wholesale price fields — this is by design and does not reflect the discount type configured in the general discount mapping.
> 3. Instruct the customer to log in as a wholesale customer on the frontend and verify that products are displaying the correct discounted price (calculated as a percentage off the regular price).
> 4. If prices are correct on the frontend, no further action is needed — the feature is working as expected.
> 5. If prices are NOT correctly discounted on the frontend, then investigate the general discount mapping settings at WooCommerce > Wholesale Prices > General Discount.
