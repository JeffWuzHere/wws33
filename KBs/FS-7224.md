# Knowledge Base Entry: Wholesale Price Reverts to Original Value When Editing Product

## 🎯 Executive Summary
**The Issue:** When editing a product's wholesale price in the admin, the new value reverts to the old price as soon as focus moves to another input field (e.g., the retail price field).
**The Fix:** This is a confirmed, replicable bug. The workaround is to save the product immediately after changing the wholesale price, before clicking into or modifying any other field.

---

## 🛠 Final Resolution (The "2nd Me" Guide)
> [!TIP]
> **Use this solution if a customer asks about:** wholesale price not saving, price reverting, price keeps changing back, wholesale price resets, product price won't update, wholesale price overwritten
>
> **Steps to solve:**
> 1. Confirm the behavior: the wholesale price field reverts when the user clicks into another input field (such as the retail/regular price) on the same product edit screen.
> 2. Inform the customer this is a **confirmed bug** (GitHub issue logged). The bug causes the wholesale price value to revert to its previous value on focus-out.
> 3. **Workaround:** When updating the wholesale price, click Save/Update immediately after entering the new price — before interacting with any other fields on the product edit page.
> 4. If the customer needs to update multiple fields, update them one at a time saving after the wholesale price change first, or set the wholesale price last before saving.
