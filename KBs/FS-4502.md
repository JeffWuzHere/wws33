# Knowledge Base Entry: Wrong Wholesale Prices Displayed With Price Caching Enabled on Multilingual Site

## 🎯 Executive Summary
**The Issue:** On a multilingual WooCommerce site with Wholesale price caching enabled, the German-language frontend was displaying incorrect wholesale prices (prices from a different wholesale role), while other languages showed correct prices; clearing the Wholesale cache restored correct prices but the issue recurred.
**The Fix:** Clearing the Wholesale price cache (Wholesale > Settings > Cache) immediately restores correct prices; for an ongoing workaround, clear the cache after any product update; the root cause is a known caching interaction that was subsequently fixed in Wholesale Prices Premium v2.0.6.

---

## 🛠 Final Resolution (The "2nd Me" Guide)
> [!TIP]
> **Use this solution if a customer asks about:** wrong prices with caching, wholesale cache bug, prices from wrong role showing, multilingual wholesale pricing wrong, WPML wholesale prices, cache clears fix prices temporarily, wholesale price caching incorrect language
>
> **Steps to solve:**
> 1. Confirm the customer has Wholesale price caching enabled (Wholesale > Settings > Cache) and is using a multilingual setup (WPML or similar).
> 2. As an immediate fix, go to Wholesale > Settings > Cache and click both "Clear Cache" buttons. Confirm whether prices are now correct on all language versions of the frontend.
> 3. Advise the customer that this is a known caching issue and that the permanent fix is included in Wholesale Prices Premium v2.0.6 or later — update the plugin to resolve it permanently.
> 4. If the customer cannot update immediately, the following workarounds prevent the issue: (a) Clear the Wholesale cache after every product update (Wholesale > Settings > Cache > Clear Cache), or (b) Disable Wholesale price caching entirely (note: this may increase page load times for logged-in B2B users).
> 5. After updating to v2.0.6+, clear the cache once more, then verify prices across all language variants and wholesale roles.

## 💡 Internal Insights (If applicable)
- This bug was specific to multilingual sites where different language versions of the same product page may be cached separately, causing cross-role price contamination in the cache.
- The immediate fix is always to clear the Wholesale price cache: Wholesale > Settings > Cache. There are two clear buttons — click both.
- Permanent fix released in Wholesale Prices Premium v2.0.6.
- This same underlying caching bug was also related to the Flatsome theme + "Show Variations Individually" issue documented in FS-5104, with the same v2.0.6 fix.
- The issue only affected frontend display prices — checkout prices remained correct regardless of cache state.
