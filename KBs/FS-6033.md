# Knowledge Base Entry: Google Site Kit Sign In With Google Compatibility with Wholesale Lead Capture

## 🎯 Executive Summary
**The Issue:** Customer wanted to know if "Sign in with Google" (via Google Site Kit) is compatible with Wholesale Lead Capture, and how Google-registered users interact with the wholesale registration flow.
**The Fix:** Google Site Kit works without issues alongside Wholesale Suite; however, users who register via "Sign in with Google" get a regular customer account — they must still go through the standard wholesale account upgrade process to become wholesale customers.

---

## 🛠 Final Resolution (The "2nd Me" Guide)
> [!TIP]
> **Use this solution if a customer asks about:** google site kit compatibility, sign in with google wholesale, google login wholesale registration, social login wholesale, google oauth wholesale lead capture
>
> **Steps to solve:**
> 1. Confirm that Google Site Kit (including the "Sign in with Google" feature) is compatible with Wholesale Suite — no known conflicts exist.
> 2. Explain the expected behavior: users who register/sign in via Google will have a standard **customer** role, not a wholesale role. The Wholesale Lead Capture registration form flow is separate.
> 3. For these Google-registered users to become wholesale customers, they need to go through the **account upgrade request** process. Direct the store owner to: https://wholesalesuiteplugin.com/kb/how-to-enable-account-upgrade-request-for-regular-customer/
> 4. Alternatively, the store admin can manually assign a wholesale role to those users from Users > All Users.

## 💡 Internal Insights (If applicable)
- "Sign in with Google" bypasses the Wholesale Lead Capture registration form entirely — it creates a WooCommerce customer account through WordPress's standard user creation flow.
- There is no automatic wholesale role assignment for social login registrations; this must be done manually by admin or via the account upgrade flow.
