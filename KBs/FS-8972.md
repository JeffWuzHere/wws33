# Knowledge Base Entry: Adding Google reCAPTCHA to the Wholesale Registration Form

## 🎯 Executive Summary
**The Issue:** Customer wanted to know how to add Google reCAPTCHA to the Wholesale Lead Capture registration form, and whether existing reCAPTCHA keys from another form on the same site could be reused.
**The Fix:** reCAPTCHA is a built-in feature in Wholesale Lead Capture; enable it under Wholesale > Settings > Wholesale Lead Capture > Security. Existing site keys and secret keys can be reused as long as the reCAPTCHA type matches.

---

## 🛠 Final Resolution (The "2nd Me" Guide)
> [!TIP]
> **Use this solution if a customer asks about:** recaptcha on wholesale registration, spam on lead capture form, bot submissions, adding captcha to trade signup form, wholesale form security
>
> **Steps to solve:**
> 1. Go to **Wholesale > Settings > Wholesale Lead Capture > Security** in the WordPress admin.
> 2. Enable the reCAPTCHA option.
> 3. Enter the Site Key and Secret Key from Google reCAPTCHA (https://www.google.com/recaptcha/admin).
> 4. If the customer already has reCAPTCHA keys in use on another form on the same site, they can reuse those same keys — but confirm the reCAPTCHA type matches (e.g., both v2 checkbox, or both v3). Mismatched types will not work.
> 5. For full setup guidance see: https://wholesalesuiteplugin.com/kb/woocommerce-wholesale-lead-capture-getting-started-guide/
