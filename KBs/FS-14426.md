# Knowledge Base Entry: New Wholesale Lead Email Notification Links to Wrong Page

## 🎯 Executive Summary
**The Issue:** The `{user_management_url}` placeholder in the New Wholesale Lead admin notification email (introduced in Wholesale Lead Capture v1.17.4) links to the default WordPress `users.php` page instead of the dedicated Wholesale Leads admin page (`wp-admin/admin.php?page=wwlc-leads-admin-page`).
**The Fix:** Replace `{user_management_url}` with `{user_edit_profile_url}` in the email template, which links directly to the individual user's profile where role assignment and approval can be performed.

---

## 🛠 Final Resolution (The "2nd Me" Guide)
> [!TIP]
> **Use this solution if a customer asks about:** wholesale lead email link wrong page, lead notification email not going to user, approve wholesale lead from email, user_management_url broken, lead approval workflow changed
>
> **Steps to solve:**
> 1. In WordPress admin, go to **WooCommerce > Settings > Emails**.
> 2. Locate the **New wholesale lead (Admin)** email and click **Manage**.
> 3. In the email body, replace the `{user_management_url}` placeholder with `{user_edit_profile_url}`.
> 4. Save the email template.
> 5. Clicking the link in future lead notification emails will now take the admin directly to that specific user's profile page for role assignment and approval.
>
> **Note:** The intended destination for `{user_management_url}` is `wp-admin/admin.php?page=wwlc-leads-admin-page#/` (Wholesale > Leads). The bug where it redirects to the default `users.php` instead has been raised with developers. The `{user_edit_profile_url}` workaround restores the previous workflow of going directly to the individual lead.
